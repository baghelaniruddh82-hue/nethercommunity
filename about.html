About Nether Community

Everything you need to know about our server

Who We Are

Nether Community is a Discord server built by Minecraft players, for Minecraft players. We started as a small group of friends back in 2024 and have grown into a thriving community of over 2,400 members. Our goal is simple — provide a welcoming space where players of all skill levels can connect, collaborate, and have fun.

Whether you're a redstone engineer, a master builder, a PvP tryhard, or someone who just likes to dig holes and chill — there's a place for you here.

Server Features

🌍 SMP Realm — Our vanilla+ survival world with quality-of-life plugins, land claiming, and a player-driven economy.

🎯 Weekly Events — UHC, build battles, spleef tournaments, and more. Suggest your own event anytime.

📚 Wiki & Guides — Community-written tutorials for farms, redstone, mods, and server mechanics.

🎨 Showcase — Share your builds, texture packs, skins, and resource packs in dedicated channels.

🏆 Ranks & Perks — Earn ranks by being active, helping others, or winning events.

Server Rules

  1. Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or bullying of any kind.
  2. No griefing or stealing on the SMP. All griefs are logged and reversible.
  3. No hacking, x-ray, or cheats on the server. Fair play only.
  4. Keep chat family-friendly. No NSFW content, spam, or excessive swearing.
  5. No advertising other servers or services without staff permission.
  6. Use channels correctly. Keep discussions in their appropriate channels.
  7. Listen to staff. Moderators and admins have the final say.
  8. Have fun! That's what we're here for.

Staff Team

Our staff team works hard to keep Nether Community safe and fun. If you need help, don't hesitate to ping a moderator or open a ticket.

We're also looking for responsible community members to join the staff team. If you're active, helpful, and level-headed, keep an eye on #announcements for when applications open.
